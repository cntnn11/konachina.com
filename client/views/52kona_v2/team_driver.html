KONACHINA 中国车队

薛威豪

Recently signed 16-year-old downhill phenom Andrew Crimmins joins the Kona Team with big goals and large aspirations. From Bredbo, New South Wales, Australia the 2013 U17 Australian National Series winner, Australian National Champion, and 16-18 Crankworx Canadian Open winner is looking forward to stepping up to the next level, racing the Junior World Cup circuit in 2014. Look for Crimmins to be a top contender for a spot on the 2014 Australian Junior Worlds Team to race the World Champs in Norway.
